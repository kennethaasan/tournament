## About the Tournament

**Vanvikan Julecup** is an annual indoor football (futsal) tournament held in Vanvikanhallen, Vanvikan, Norway. The tournament features:

- 5v5 indoor football on artificial turf
- 12-minute matches
- Separate men's and women's divisions
- Group stage followed by knockout rounds
- Age requirement: 15+ years

The tournament has been running since at least 2012, with both Christmas (Julecup) and Easter (Påskecup) editions.
